RSI (Relative Strength Index)

It is a Technical Indicator which is used to analysis the strength of a stock or financial instruments. It was developed by J. Welles Wilder in 1978. It is a momentum indicator which move within a range of 0 to 100 in which range above 70 shows an overbought zone and as per technical analyst stock price might drop down also range below 30 shown and oversold zone and as per technical analyst stock price might bounce back and move upward but it is not compulsory.

MACD (Moving Average Convergence Divergence)

MACD is a momentum indicator which calculate the difference between mvoing averages and show possibility of movement for both side. It was developed by Gerald Appel in the late 1970s. The MACD is the subtraction value of 26 period EMA (Exponential moving average) from 12 period EMA (Exponential moving average). The result of the subtraction value will be shown as a black line which is also called as "MACD LINE". Also a 9 period EMA (Exponential moving average) is called as "SIGNAL LINE".

Whenever the "MACD LINE" cross over the "SIGNAL LINE" it indicates a Bullish sentiment and if "MACD LINE" cross below the "SIGNAL LINE" it indicates a Bearish sentiment.
